Steps to USDA Eligibility

  1. Form AD-2047, Customer Data Worksheet
    Completed by individuals or legal entities who have not previously provided identifying information to USDA.
  2. Form CCC-860, Socially Disadvantaged, Limited Resource, Beginning and Veteran Farmer or Rancher Certification
    Used to certify membership in qualifying producer groups.
  3. Establish a Farm Record and Obtain a Farm Number
    Required for USDA program participation. Documentation such as a deed, lease, or heirs’ property verification may be needed.
  4. Form AD-1026, Highly Erodible Land Conservation and Wetland Conservation Certification
    Required for most USDA programs. Applicants with farming interests must complete the full form.
  5. Form CCC-941, Average Adjusted Gross Income (AGI) Certification
    Certifies that the applicant’s AGI does not exceed $900,000.
  6. Form CCC-902I, Farm Operating Plan for an Individual
    Provides information on the structure and contributions to the farming operation.
  7. Form SF-3881, Payment Enrollment Form for FSA
    Used to set up or update direct deposit information for USDA payments.
  8. Form SF-1199A, Payment Enrollment Form for NRCS
    Used to set up or update direct deposit information for USDA NRCS payments.
