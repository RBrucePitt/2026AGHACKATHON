Getting started with USDA

  • 1  Create your USDA account
    Fill out Form AD 2047 to create your account in the USDA system.
  • 12  Create a Farm Record
    Provide proof of land ownership or control. You will receive a USDA farm number, which is required when filing your acreage report and other forms.
  • 13  Land preservation
    Fill out Form AD 1026 to certify compliance with Highly Erodible Land and Wetland Conservation requirements for USDA program eligibility.
  • 14  Income qualification
    Fill out Form CCC 941 to Certify that your average adjusted gross income does not exceed the USDA limit of $900,000.
  • 15  Farm operation plan
    Fill out Form CCC 902 to describe your farm’s structure, labor, land, and capital contributions. This determines payment eligibility.
  • 16  (Optional) Qualify for special assistance
    Fill out Form CCC-860 if you are a socially disadvantaged, limited resource, beginning, or veteran farmer or rancher. This could give you access to more grants.
  • 17  Setup direct deposit to your bank account
    Fill out Form SF 3881 to receive FSA grant payments.
    Fill out Form SF 1199A to receive NRCS payments.

Now you are ready to file your acreage report, and get paid!
